1) Please create a Pandas DataFrame with which to show the consumption of each year. The expected format is to have the year number as column name and mm-dd as index.

2) Please also create a seasonal plot showing 5-years (2016-2020) range (shaded) & average (dashed line), and year 2021 (line) & 2022 (line).

3) Please comment on your observation on the plot

In [54]:
combine = pd.DataFrame(index=df['mm-dd'].unique())
combine.reset_index(inplace=True)
combine.rename(columns={'index':'mm-dd'}, inplace=True)

for year in df['yyyy'].unique():
    temp = df[df['yyyy'] == year]
    temp = temp[['Consumption', 'mm-dd']].rename(columns={'Consumption':str(year)})
    combine = combine.merge(temp, on='mm-dd', how='left', validate='one_to_one')

combine = combine.set_index('mm-dd')

Observations¶

1) Overall each yearly trend is broadly the same

2) Consumption generally follows annual seasonality: High in Winter, low in Summer

3) Towards the end of 2021 consumption falls below the average and this trend has continued for almost all of 2022

4) 2022 is also now marking the lowest range of consumption in Q3 ever, not surprising following the hot summer
